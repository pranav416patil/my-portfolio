My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Aavtar

I thoroughly enjoyed this movie due to its intriguing concept, stunning visuals, and captivating storyline. The film masterfully presents the fascinating Hindu mythology theme of "Aavtar," wherein a single soul inhabits two separate physical bodies. The stunning graphics, animations, and special effects are truly awe-inspiring, especially when viewed in 3D. Additionally, the film successfully conveys a profound message about the dangerous consequences of human greed for wealth, making it highly relevant to our modern times.

3 Idiots

This Indian film sheds light on the lives of engineering students and their efforts to achieve excellent academic scores, which they believe will secure them good job opportunities upon graduation. However, amidst this pursuit, there is a student named Rancho who values acquiring practical skills over merely chasing grades. Ultimately, the movie highlights the importance of skills and their relevance for thriving in the real world.

Bhool Bhulaiyaa

This Indian Hindi movie is a remake of the 1993 Malayalam film "Manichitrathazhu combining elements of psychology, suspense, and comedy. You can watch it any number of times without getting bored.
